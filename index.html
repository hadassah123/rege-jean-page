About Regé-Jean Page

Regé-Jean Page is an acclaimed British actor best known for his leading role as Simon Basset, the Duke of Hastings, in the debut season of Netflix's "Bridgerton." Born in London and trained at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA), he has established himself as a versatile performer across stage, television, and film. Known for bringing depth, elegance, and authenticity to his roles, Regé-Jean continues to pursue diverse, challenging projects that explore the human condition.
